Important Event Policy

DUE TO INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS, ANIMALS CANNOT ATTEND THIS YEAR'S PARADE OR PICNIC, INCLUDING DOGS AND HORSES.

This is clearly very disappointing and not consistent with the spirit of Monte Nido, but with the short notice to pull everything together by just a couple of neighbors taking initiative, it added too much complexity to solve this year. If any neighbor would like to step up and take ownership of this to figure out how to include horses, please don't hesitate to reach out via the Volunteer button and you can join us. We would welcome it!

Thank you for your understanding.
